Our Story


"Ujjaval Samaj Bhalai Sanstha" to facilitate the women of the country to make extra money and get financially independent, the government has started this "USBS". Being as a part of professionalism, the training becomes more effective for the girls. Here we teach the concepts of stitching and beautician which are very useful for the future.

Ujjaval Samaj Bhalai Sanstha who being a guiding force behind the above achievements has been actively involved in higher-end training at its own premise. This Institute feels proud on contributing in our nation.

We have started two basic courses [ Stitching and Beautician ]for the girls. Our goal is to teach you what you need to know, using experienced and friendly instructors.
